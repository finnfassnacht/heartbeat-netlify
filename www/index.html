First Aid

If unconscious

  • Tilt the head back, Push the chin forward
  • Place ear over mouth, Look at chest
  • Listen for sound, See chest rise and fall

If breathing

  • Lay arm at 90° angle, palm facing up
  • Lay other arm over chest, against the cheek
  • Grab the thigh, bend the leg
  • Pull patient towards you, Align thigh at 90° angle to hip
  • Align hand to Overextend neck

If not breathing

  • Overextend neck, Pinch nose shut
  • Seal the mouth with yours
  • Deliver first breath
  • Take a deep breath
  • Deliver second breath
  • Locate the lower edge of rib cage
  • Place the tip of middle finger in notch
    • and against the bottom of the sternum.
  • Place index finger next to middle finger
  • Place heel of hand next to middle
  • Interlock your hands and push down hard (4 to 5cm)
  • Realease pressure completely
  • Continue cylce in a 100-120 bpm rythmen
    • Think of "Ah Ah Ah" from "Stayin' Alive"
  • Stop Cylce after 30 repetitions and deliver breaths
